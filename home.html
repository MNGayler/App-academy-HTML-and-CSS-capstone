Welcome to the fruit farm

The Farm

Our farm is located at the in the Blue hills valley, between the Aqua Ocean and the Blue Hills. The farm has been with the family for five generations. We always take pride in growing and selling the best sun ripened fruit.

Our Fruit

Our fruit is always organic and is ripened by our long summers and kept fresh by the cool sea breeze.

Feel free to buy our fruit by registering with us. You can also come by and visit us and pick your own soft fruits. We look forward to seeing you soon.
